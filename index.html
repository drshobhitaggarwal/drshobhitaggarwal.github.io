About Me

I'm Dr. Shobhit Aggarwal. My academic path began in Panipat, Haryana, India, where I earned my Bachelor of Technology from Kurukshetra University in 2014. Driven by my interest in electronics and communication, I then moved to the United States.

I continued my studies at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, completing my Master's in 2018 and my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering in 2023.

Professionally, I've served as an Embedded Firmware Engineer in an IoT-based engineering firm in Charlotte and as a Senior Embedded Software Engineer at a Fortune 500 electrical corporation.

My passion for teaching and research led me to transition from industry to academia. My research interests include IoT, Wireless Communication, Embedded Systems, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence. I frequently present my work at international conferences.

When I'm not working, you can find me hiking in the mountains, making new devices, and experimenting with new recipes. I believe in continuous growth, both professionally and personally.
